How do I use the AI assistant?

                
Type your question in natural language in the chat input. For example: "Which projects are over budget this month?" or "Show me revenue by client for Q1."

              

              

                
What kind of questions can I ask?

                
You can ask about project budgets, revenue, utilisation, invoicing, expenses, team capacity, and more. The assistant translates your question into a report against your live Projectworks data.

              

              

                
What is Agent mode vs Ask mode?

                
Agent mode allows the assistant to take multi-step actions and build reports autonomously. Ask mode is a simpler Q&A for quick one-off questions.

              

              

                
Can I save or export reports?

                
Reports generated in a session are saved to your chat history. Export functionality will be available in a future update.

              

              

                
Who can see my reports?

                
Reports are generated against your organisation's data and are only visible to users with access to your Projectworks workspace.

              

              

                
How do I find a previous report?

                
Use the search bar in the sidebar to find past conversations, or scroll through your recent chat history.
